about us

Ufotable, Inc. is a Japanese animation studio founded in October 2000 by former staff of TMS Entertainment through its subsidiary Telecom Animation Film and located in Nakano, Tokyo Prefecture. A distinctive feature seen in many of their works is clay animation.
